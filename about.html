OUR STORY

We started the Caffeine Lounge back in 2010 with the idea that we wanted to create a space where people can come awake and come alive simply through having a sip of our bomb.com coffee. We pride ourselves in working with farmers in Mexico and Guatemala who care about coffee just as much as we do. Also its good to note that we buy our coffee fair trade. So feel good knowing that throughout the entire process of get this beautiful caffeinated drink into your hands, it was all done at a fair price benefiting everyone involved. We wanted to be apart of a coffee that not only tastes good but has a good foundation with our ethos in how its made and cared for. The Caffeine Lounge is also about taking it slow and providing the perfect environment to get your work done or catch up with some friends.

Our staff at the Caffeine Lounge care about creating a culture that is not only inviting, warm and friendly but also one where you can feel productive but also be super chill at the same time. We strive to create a space that is bright, light and airy where you can feel free to come and go as you please. All of our staff are committed to creating the ultimate experience which boils down to the quality of our coffee beans and how they are roasted all the way to our latte art, which hopefully puts a smile on your face. We care not only about the caffeine that fuels you to be your best self but also about making sure you are apart of our family, staying as strong as our coffee.
